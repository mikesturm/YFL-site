Here is the complete list of my series "Oblique: Week by Week", which breaks down a randomly picked Oblique Strategy---a concept created by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt. The strategies were created as a way to help those who create get better a doing it---largely by aiding in overcoming creative blocks. My aim with this project is to do the same---breaking down what I take each of the aphorisms to mean. My hope is that I can help a few people do better creative work in the process. For more information, see the introductory post . The Oblique Strategies are copyright Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt, all rights reserved.
